About the 3D Era

The 3D era of gaming, spanning the late 1990s and early 2000s, marked a revolutionary shift from 2D to 3D graphics. Consoles like the PlayStation 1, Nintendo 64, and Sega Dreamcast pushed the boundaries of technology, delivering immersive worlds and groundbreaking gameplay experiences. Relive the era that defined modern gaming.
