Featured Consoles

NES

The NES revolutionized gaming in the 1980s with iconic titles like Super Mario Bros. and The Legend of Zelda.

Sega Genesis

Known for its "Blast Processing" and classics like Sonic the Hedgehog, the Genesis was a fierce competitor in the 16-bit era.

Super Famicom

The Super Famicom (SNES) brought us timeless games like Super Metroid and Chrono Trigger.

About the Retro Era

The retro era of Japanese gaming, spanning the 1980s and early 1990s, was a time of innovation and creativity. From the rise of Nintendo to the fierce competition with Sega, this era laid the foundation for modern gaming. Relive the nostalgia of pixelated graphics, chiptune music, and groundbreaking gameplay.
